Ha? Laman

iACADEMY Unity Game Jam

2020 | PC | UI/UX Developer | Unity & C#

Sync or Sink

ESGS: Game On 2018 Entry

2019 | Android Mobile AR, Tabletop | Project Manager, UI/UX Designer & Artist | Adobe Photoshop

Sync or Sink is an AR tabletop game for android that puts you in the midst of a natural disaster. Unprepared and with the first floor of your home flooded, you and your family rush to around your home, following the DCRMC’s instructions to prepare a 72 hour disaster kit. With the flood rising at an alarming rate, you must gather and build what you can with the help of the DCRMC to survive!
